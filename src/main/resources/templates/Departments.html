🏫 Our Departments

Our school offers a well-structured academic and co-curricular system through various departments that focus on overall student development.

📚 Academic Departments

  • Science: Encourages scientific thinking and practical learning.
  • Mathematics: Builds logical reasoning and problem-solving skills.
  • Languages: Improves communication skills in English and other languages.
  • Social Studies: Provides knowledge about history, geography, and society.
  • Computer Science: Develops digital skills and basic programming knowledge.

🎯 Other Departments

  • Sports: Promotes physical fitness and teamwork through games and activities.
  • Arts & Cultural Activities: Encourages creativity through drawing, music, and dance.
  • Administration (Office): Manages school operations, records, and student services.

These departments work together to ensure students achieve academic excellence along with personal growth and discipline.
